Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 18, 2024

Summary: RestEasy stores all your photos locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or have access to any of your data. Your privacy is absolute.

What Data We Collect

None. RestEasy does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your device.

Photo Storage

All photos you capture in RestEasy are stored exclusively on your device in the app's private storage. Photos are:

  • Never uploaded to any server
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never accessible to us or anyone else
  • Automatically deleted based on your timer settings

Photo Deletion

RestEasy automatically deletes your photos at the time you configure. You can also manually delete any photo at any time from within the app by tapping the Delete button on any check. Once deleted, photos cannot be recovered.

On-Device Preferences

RestEasy uses on-device storage (UserDefaults) to save your preferences, such as your auto-delete time. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Camera Access

RestEasy requests camera access solely to capture photos within the app. Camera access is not used for any other purpose.

Purchases

If you purchase RestEasy, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

RestEasy does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

RestEasy does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version here with an updated effective date.
